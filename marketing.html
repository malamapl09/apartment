Everything you need

A complete platform for every role

Twelve integrated modules covering every aspect of residential building management, from finances to visitor access.

Dashboard Analytics

Real-time insights into collection rates, maintenance trends, occupancy, and visitor statistics across your building.

Apartment Management

Track units, assign owners, manage occupancy status and comprehensive unit details in a centralized registry.

Resident Directory

Complete owner and resident profiles with emergency contacts, communication preferences, and apartment links.

Common Spaces

Configure amenities with pricing, booking rules, availability schedules, capacity limits, and blackout date management.

Reservation System

Full booking flow with payment verification, approval workflows, conflict detection, and automatic cancellation of unpaid holds.

Fees & Payments

Generate charges, track payment status, record transactions across multiple payment methods, and automate overdue detection.

Maintenance Requests

Submit, assign, and track requests with priority levels, categories, photo attachments, and real-time status updates.

Visitor Management

Register visitors with unique access codes, check-in and check-out tracking, and support for recurring visitor schedules.

Package Tracking

Log incoming deliveries, automatically notify residents, and track pickup status from arrival to collection.

Polls & Voting

Create polls with single or multiple choice formats, publish results on a schedule, and support anonymous voting for sensitive topics.

Document Library

Categorized document storage for building rules, meeting minutes, contracts, legal notices, and resident-facing forms.

Announcements

Building-wide notices with targeted audience segmentation, priority levels, and automated email delivery to all residents.
